Lamb 'n' Rice Stuffed Cabbage Rolls

Description

These cabbage rolls are stuffed with a mixture of ground lamb, rice, and spices, then simmered in a tangy tomato sauce. They're a delicious and comforting meal that's perfect for a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 1 head cabbage
  • 1 pound ground lamb
  • 1/2 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add cabbage and cook for 2 minutes. Remove cabbage from water and let cool. Remove 12 large leaves from cabbage; set aside. Chop remaining cabbage and place in the bottom of a large pot.
  2. In a large bowl, combine lamb, rice, onion, parsley, allspice, cinnamon, pepper, and salt. Mix well. Place about 1/4 cup of meat mixture in the center of each cabbage leaf. Roll up, tucking in the sides, and place rolls on top of chopped cabbage in pot.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, lemon juice, and sugar. Pour over cabbage rolls. Place a heatproof plate on top of rolls to keep them submerged in liquid.
  4. Cover pot and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 1 1/2 hours, or until meat is cooked through and rice is tender. Serve hot.
